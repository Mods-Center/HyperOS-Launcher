# Launcher Mod v6 - FAQ

---

### **Q: I am not getting glassy blur**
**A:** Glassy blur is implemented in framework of OS3, hence it can't be enabled in HyperOS 2. And if you are on HyperOS 3 your rom or device must support glassy blur via advanced release prop value being 4

---

### **Q: I I am not getting background for folders.**  
**A:** This happens when your device doesn't support blur natively.Use modules like anti low end which unlocks blur for your device.

---

### **Q: My Circle to Search isn't working.**  
**A:** There's an overlay in the module for unsupported ROMs. Make sure it's installed and enable "Circle to Search" from Navigation Settings.

---

### **Q: When I long press on any icon it crashes**  
**A:** It is because of Lsposed modules in most cases, make sure to turn off hook for launcher if you are using any module to customise launcher externally

---

### **Q: Are there 1x2 and 2x1 grids for folders?**  
**A:** Yes.

---

### **Q: Can I use this module in a tablet (Pad)?**  
**A:** The mod was built for mobile. It hasn't been tested in tablets.
